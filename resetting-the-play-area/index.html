    Resetting the play area

    If the play area (Guardian) is incorrect or the headset has been moved to a new room, you may need to reset the play area.

    This can be done directly from the Meta Quest menu inside the headset.

    Opening the Quest menu

    1. Put on the VR headset.
    2. Press the Meta (Oculus) button on the right controller.
    3. The Quest menu will appear.

    Resetting the play area

    1. Open the Quick Settings panel.
    2. Select Boundary.
    3. Choose Reset Boundary or Redraw Boundary.
    4. Follow the instructions in the headset.
      See Setting up the play area.

    When to reset the play area

    You should reset the play area if:

    • The Guardian appears in the wrong position
    • The headset was moved to another room
    • The training area is too small
    • The headset asks to set up a new boundary
